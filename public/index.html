Sustainability Tips

Why Plant-Based Recipes?

Plant-based diets are not only nutritious but also have a lower environmental footprint. They reduce greenhouse gas emissions, save water, and require fewer natural resources compared to animal-based dishes.

How the Sustainability Score Works

Every recipe is scored from 1 to 10 based on ingredient sustainability, considering local sourcing, seasonality, and organic practices. A higher score means a more planet-friendly dish.

How to eliminate wastage of kitchen scraps

Composting kitchen scraps turns food waste into nutrient-rich fertilizer, replacing energy-intensive synthetic fertilizers,leads to no waste of food,directly shrinking your carbon footprint.
